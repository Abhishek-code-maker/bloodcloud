Why Should Donate Blood ?

Blood Donation Is The Act Of Giving Blood To Someone Who Needs It. It Is Not Just About Giving Blood,But It
Is An Act Of Kindness That Saves The Lives Of Hundreds Of People. These Fifteen Minutes Of Your LifeCan Save
